About Me



My name is Kaylyn Cheatham, and I am currently an Information Developer (technical writer and editor) and User Experience professional for IBM in the Columbus, Ohio area. I write, graphic design, and edit documentation for the IBM Watson platform. I also curate and write blogs for the Watson developer section of IBM's technical journal, IBM developerWorks.

I have a passion for education, and for giving underserved people and women the opportunity for better education. Towards this goal, I volunteer with a group of coworkers for an organization called Tech Corps. Tech Corps sends groups of technology professionals into underserved schools in the community to run a 'Techie Club'. Techie Club is designed to introduce elementary school children to the fundamentals of computer programming, robotics, web development and digital media through fun and interactive sessions. We meet after school weekly, throughout the school year.
